# 🛺 QuickRick  

> Drivers see demand. Passengers see supply.  
> A real-time visibility platform for faster, smarter urban mobility.

---

## The Problem  

Auto-rickshaw drivers often spend hours roaming without passengers, burning fuel, losing money, and relying purely on chance. At the same time, passengers stand roadside waiting with no visibility into whether an auto is nearby.  

This creates a daily urban inefficiency:

### Drivers:
- Waste fuel searching blindly  
- Lose time in low-demand areas  
- Earn less due to idle roaming  

### Passengers:
- Wait unnecessarily  
- Have no idea where nearby autos are  
- Face uncertainty during peak hours  

---

## The Solution  

**QuickRick** bridges this visibility gap through a live, map-based demand system.

Instead of traditional booking, QuickRick focuses on **real-time location intelligence**:

### Passenger Side:
- Open the app and view nearby autos in real time  
- Drop a one-tap demand signal at your location  
- Become instantly visible to nearby drivers  
- No login, no booking queue, no friction  

### Driver Side:
- Open the app and view passenger demand zones  
- Red dots show where passengers are waiting  
- Heat zones indicate high-demand areas  
- Drive directly toward demand instead of roaming randomly  

---

## Core Idea  

**No commissions. No middleman. No forced bookings.**  

QuickRick is designed as a **visibility-first platform** — making supply and demand visible so both sides save time and money.

---

## Key Features (v1)

✅ Live auto visibility for passengers  
✅ Live passenger demand signals for drivers  
✅ Auto-expiring demand signals (10 minutes)  
✅ Heat-map clustering for busy locations  
✅ Lightweight, zero-login access  
✅ Faster passenger-driver discovery
